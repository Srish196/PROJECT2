Varun Smriti Park

Varun Smriti Park has a beautiful story behind it. Made in the memory of a young Varun, who lost his life at an age of 10 years- this park is an initiative by his family to keep his interests alive forever. Literally translating to; in the memory of Varun, this is a beautiful park with swimming pool and a full-fledged water park for families to enjoy.
