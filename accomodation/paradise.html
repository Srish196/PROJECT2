Paradise Hotel

This hotel in Sagar offers swimming pool, complimentary breakfast and Wi-Fi access to its guests and is at a walking distance from the Khyati Restaurant. This hotel has 50 well-appointed rooms, spread across 4 floors. Air conditioner, television, minifridge, sofa, balcony and attached bathroom with hot/cold water supply are some of the conveniences offered in the room. Guest can avail various facilities that include front desk, banquet facility, conference facility, garden and parking. Laundry, ironing, doctor on call and room service are also offered for the convenience of the guests. The hotel in Sagar is accessible via travel hub such as Makronia Railway Station (1 km). The closest airport to this property is Dhana Airport (19.5 km). Some of the tourist attractions are Lakha Banjara Lake (8 km) and Gadpahra fort (16 km). Guest can dine in at the in-house restaurant and can enjoy drinks at the stocked bar within the premises
