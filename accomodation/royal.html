Royal Palace

Location: Royal Palace is nestled in Sagar which is a very beautiful and picturesque destination Royal Palace is a well worth property that proffers a customized accommodation at an affordable rate. Easy mode of transportation to the hotel is available through Dumna Airport and Sagar Railway station(8 km) that are well connected to all major cities. Room Amenities: The rooms of the hotel are lavishly furnished with a liberating sense of space ensuring comfort and convenience even to the most descending guest. The rooms are well equipped with modern facilities like air conditioner, television, tea/coffee maker, room service. Every room has attached washroom with hot/cold water supply and essential toiletries. Hotel Facilities: The hotel offers myriad of conveniences like Wi-Fi, laundry, pickup and drop facility, doctor on call and many more for the magnificent stay of guests. It has a backup generator within the property in case of power failure. All major credit cards are accepted to provide patrons a relaxed and hassle free stay. The hotel is well equipped with a business center which offers private meeting rooms with a focus on comfort and convenience, state-of-the-art technology and impeccable services. Dining: The multi cuisine restaurant serves choicest dishes from around the world to cater to the varied taste of travelers.
