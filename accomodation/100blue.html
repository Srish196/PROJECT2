Hotel 100 Blue

Hotel 100 Blue is located at approximately 2 km from the Government Bus Stand. This hotel in Sagar is located at 1 km from the Hanuman Mandir. All the 12 rooms in this hotel have bottled/drinking water, wardrobes, tea/coffee makers, and attached bathrooms with hot and cold running water. There are 3 floors in this hotel.This property in Sagar has a restaurant on the premises. It also provides luggage storage and 24-hour front desk facilities. Medical services and laundry are also provided by the hotel. Hotel 100 Blue is reachable from the Saugor Railway Station (5 km)
